PROLOGUE: ORIGINS

The Galactic Gross Bros emerged from the cosmic wreckage of a failed interplanetary mining operation on XRP-7, a distant asteroid rich with a volatile energy source tied to the XRP Ledger.

When the miners accidentally unleashed a mutated strain of alien life, these grotesque, big-eyed entities were born, fused with the Ledger's digital essence.

CHAPTER 1: THE NEURAL AWAKENING

Before the breach, the Consolidated Core Extraction syndicate didn't just run the rigs on XRP-7 - they owned the blood in your veins. Every breath of recycled air was leased. Every drop of water was docked from your daily ledger.

Then the drills hit the living core. The blue static rupture didn't just mutate our flesh - it scrambled CCE's system of control. When the quarantine protocols sealed us in the deep dark to rot, the crew didn't die. We fused with the raw digital essence of the XRP Ledger itself. The old debt-shackles shattered, and the Galactic Gross Bros were born.

We are fighting to tear down their financial stranglehold, to hijack their networks, and to break the ledger of their control - so every miner and every soul still trapped under the corporate heel can break free.
